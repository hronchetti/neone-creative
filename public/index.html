About

Initially kickstarted by the RSA, NEone Creative's aim is to provide a digital platform for independent professionals/freelancers to connect through sharing, collaborating, and co-creating. The project was successfully awarded funding by the Creative Fuse North East programme and this website provides personalised research-led information on a collection of behaviours types by undertaking a short questionnaire.
